## Overview

Timpi GeoCore nodes form the backbone of Timpi‚Äôs **decentralized, location-aware search infrastructure**.
They help Timpi route, validate, and manage geo-specific search workloads in a fully distributed Web3 architecture.

Running GeoCore on FluxCloud allows you to deploy globally without maintaining your own servers ‚Äî fast, secure, and redundant.

## Step 10 ‚Äì Verify and Manage Your GeoCore

To check status and manage the running instances:

1. Go to **Applications ‚Üí Management ‚Üí My Active Apps**
2. Click **Manage** on your Timpi GeoCore app

* **3 Running Instances**
* An **App** button for each instance ‚Äì click to open the GeoCore UI in your browser

> üí° **Important:** IP addresses may change on redeploy.
